The Story

Gran Turismo is an action-packed movie that takes you on a thrilling journey through the world of high-stakes racing. Follow the adventures of our protagonist as they strive to become the ultimate racing champion.

Meet the Characters

Jann Mardenborough, a Gran Turismo player who dreams of becoming a real-life racecar driver, is played by Archie Madekwe. The actor is best known for his promising collaborations with renowned director Ari Aster, starring in two of his latest movies: Midsommar and Beau Is Afraid
